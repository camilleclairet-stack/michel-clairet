About

A journey through France's finest kitchens — into London's private households.

Trained in classical French cuisine and pâtisserie, Michel began his career at Georges Blanc, three Michelin stars, before a decade at the Majestic Hotel in Cannes. Since 2008, he has served as private head chef to families in Holland Park, Knightsbridge and Chelsea.
